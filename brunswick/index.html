Chapter 05 — In conversation

Julian Aiken

Founder & Managing Partner

It started around the game.
Then I learned the business of it.

"Access without intention is just a story to tell at a party. Access with intention is an education you can't buy."

Julian grew up around the NBA — his uncle played seven years in the league. He spent nearly two years working in the industry for free, learning the business cold. In 2018, one yes turned into six clients.

Today, Brunswick manages 35+. Julian is the relationship — but his commitment to every athlete is backed by a full team running finance, content, community, and day-to-day. Nothing depends on one person alone.

Before you ask

Questions about Brunswick.

We work with professional athletes across the NFL, NBA, and MLB — and increasingly with NIL athletes entering the pros. Every relationship starts with an introduction.

It means we keep the roster small on purpose. Every client works directly with Julian and a senior team — never handed to a junior associate or left in a CRM queue.

Most agencies focus on contract negotiation and endorsements. Brunswick focuses on everything around the career — finance, brand, community, and daily operations. We work alongside agents and advisors, not instead of them.

Most relationships start through a referral from a player, agent, or advisor already in the Brunswick network. If you believe you have a connection, reach out directly at hello@brunswick.co.
