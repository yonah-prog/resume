Brunswick is the operating partner that makes your client's career run cleanly. We don't compete with you — we make your job easier, and your client better served.

Working with agents

You negotiate the deal. We make sure everything around it works.

Your job is contract negotiation and team relationships. That's where your time belongs. Brunswick handles the rest — finance, content, community, and day-to-day — so your client is organized, visible, and set up to maximize every dollar you put in their contract.

When a client's personal life is running smoothly and their brand is growing, they're less distracted, less stressed, and better positioned for the next negotiation.

We've worked alongside agents at CAA, Roc Nation Sports, Priority Sports, and independent firms. We complement the team — we never compete with it.

  • Clean financials fed directly to your client's advisor
  • Brand momentum that increases contract leverage
  • Less noise from your client between seasons
  • A full team behind the relationship, not just one person
Working with advisors

Clean books. Real-time numbers. No surprises.

The biggest challenge most advisors face with athlete clients is data quality. Transactions spread across ten accounts, entities that haven't been reconciled in years, income that was never properly categorized.

Brunswick feeds your practice clean, reconciled financials in real time. Every entity, every account, every month — closed on time, categorized correctly.

You make better investment decisions when you have better data. We're the infrastructure that makes that possible.

  • Monthly close — books done by the 10th
  • Entity map — full structure documented
  • Tax coordination — we work alongside your team
  • Real-time access — portal login for your team
