The story

Building a brand in the NFL's biggest market.

Tyler Nubin came into the NFL in 2024 as a pick of the New York Giants. He came into the NFL's largest market with a clean slate and a clear ambition: to build a brand worthy of the jersey. Brunswick is managing that build, deliberately, from the ground up.

The work started immediately. Brunswick placed Nubin at a high-profile luxury real estate event alongside Louis XII, Mr Porter, and The 74 — the kind of room that defines New York taste-making. The New York Post covered it. The signal was intentional: this is a player who belongs in these conversations, not just on game day.

The full brand operation runs through Brunswick's content infrastructure. Assets, strategy, and platform management are coordinated through @brunswick.sm, where Nubin's story is being built one well-placed moment at a time. New York rewards athletes who show up with intention. Tyler Nubin is showing up.
