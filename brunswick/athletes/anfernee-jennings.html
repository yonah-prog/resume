The story

Community work that made national news.

Anfernee Jennings doesn't make a lot of noise. He makes a lot of difference. The New England Patriots linebacker is one of the most community-oriented players on the roster — and in November 2024, he put that on full display alongside teammate DeMario Douglas.

Brunswick coordinated the Thanksgiving Turkey Drive: a structured effort across six Massachusetts Boys & Girls Clubs that put 1,500 turkeys in the hands of families who needed them. Sports Illustrated ran the story. Brunswick was named as the organizer. For Jennings, it wasn't a PR play — it was who he is. Brunswick's job was to make sure the work was seen.

That's the Brunswick model for community initiatives: pair the athlete's genuine commitment with operational infrastructure and press strategy. Jennings brought the intention. Brunswick brought the logistics, the coordination, and the story. The result was a national feature that anchored both athletes' public profiles heading into the 2024 season.
