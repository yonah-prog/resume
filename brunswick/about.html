The firm

Built around the athlete.
Run like a business.

Brunswick is a boutique sports management firm operating at the intersection of finance, content, community, and concierge. Founded by Julian Aiken in 2018, we manage 35+ professional athletes across the NFL, NBA, and MLB.

How it started
Julian Aiken / Founder

It started around the game. Then I learned the business of it.

"Access without intention is just a story to tell at a party. Access with intention is an education you can't buy."

Julian grew up around the NBA — his uncle played seven years in the league. He spent nearly two years working in the industry for free, learning the business cold. Every meeting, every deal, every conversation was an investment in understanding how this world actually works.

In 2018, one yes turned into six clients. Today, Brunswick manages 35+ athletes across the NFL, NBA, and MLB — with a team running finance, content, community, and day-to-day operations behind every one of them.

Julian is the relationship. But his commitment to every athlete is backed by a full team — so nothing depends on one person alone.
