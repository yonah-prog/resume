Queens Infusion Center

Expert IV therapy and compassionate care — conveniently located in Queens, Fresh Meadows, close to home.

Your Queens care center

At our Queens infusion center, we deliver personalized IV therapy and hematology-oncology care in a comfortable, welcoming environment. Our board-certified team tailors every treatment plan to your specific needs — no long hospital waits required.

We perform treatments on-site with an in-house lab, so you get faster results and spend less time waiting. Next-day appointments are available for most services.

Services at this location

✓Iron infusion therapy for iron-deficiency anemia
✓Chemotherapy and immunotherapy administration
✓Wellness and hydration infusions
✓Injectable medications and biologics
✓On-site lab work and monitoring
