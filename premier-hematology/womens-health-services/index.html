Our Services

Women's Health Services Built Around Your Needs

We understand the unique health challenges women face at every stage of life. From iron deficiency to hormonal imbalances, our women's health services are designed to help you feel your best.

Hematology Care for Women at Every Stage

At Premier Hematology, we understand that women's health needs evolve throughout life's journey. Our dedicated team is here to provide expert care and support for all your hematology concerns. Partnering with Ob/Gyn physicians, we address a range of issues, including clotting disorders, abnormal blood counts, and iron deficiency associated with heavy menstrual bleeding. Whether you're preparing for motherhood or managing your health through menopause, we're committed to helping you thrive.

Pregnancy & Iron

Our team specializes in ensuring that expectant mothers have adequate iron levels to support both their own health and the development of their babies. Intravenous iron is a safe and easy way to increase iron and hemoglobin levels quickly to ensure a healthy pregnancy. Detection is easy with routine blood work and early treatment is key to avoid a blood transfusion during birth.

Menstrual Health & Iron Deficiency

From managing heavy menstrual bleeding to addressing symptoms of iron deficiency, our comprehensive approach to women's health focuses on empowering you to live your best life. Whether you're experiencing weakness, fatigue, or other symptoms of low iron, our team is here to provide expert care and support every step of the way.

Bariatric Surgery Support

For those undergoing bariatric surgery, proper hematology management is crucial to support overall wellness and ensure a successful outcome. Our team works closely with bariatric specialists to provide comprehensive care tailored to your unique needs.
