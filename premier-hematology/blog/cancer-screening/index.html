Cancer Screening

Premier Hematology & Oncology
2024 · 5 min read

Early Detection for Effective Treatment

Cancer screenings play a vital role in detecting cancer at its earliest stages, when treatment is most effective. At Premier Hematology, we offer comprehensive cancer screening services designed to detect various types of cancer at the earliest possible stage. Our screenings are tailored to your age, gender, and individual risk factors, ensuring that you receive the most appropriate screening tests for your specific needs.

Tailored Screening Services

Breast Cancer: Regular mammograms are essential for the early detection of breast cancer in women. Our screening protocols are designed to detect breast cancer at its earliest stages, when treatment options are most effective.

Cervical Cancer

Routine Pap tests are recommended for the early detection of cervical cancer in women. Our screening program includes Pap tests and HPV testing to identify abnormalities in cervical cells and detect the presence of high-risk HPV strains.

Colorectal Cancer

Colorectal cancer screening is recommended for both men and women starting at age 45 or earlier if you have certain risk factors. Our screening methods include colonoscopy, fecal occult blood tests, and stool DNA tests to detect precancerous polyps and early-stage colorectal cancer.

Prostate Cancer

Prostate cancer screening is recommended for men starting at age 50 or earlier if you have a family history of prostate cancer. Our screening program includes prostate-specific antigen (PSA) blood tests and digital rectal exams to detect prostate cancer in its early stages.

Lung Cancer

Lung cancer screening is recommended for current and former smokers aged 55 to 80 who have a history of heavy smoking. Our screening program uses low-dose CT scans to detect lung cancer in its early stages, when it is most treatable.

Importance of Early Detection

Early detection of cancer through routine screenings can significantly improve your chances of successful treatment and survival. By detecting cancer at its earliest stages, we can often treat the disease more effectively and increase the likelihood of a positive outcome. Regular screenings also allow us to identify precancerous lesions and other abnormalities before they develop into cancer, enabling us to intervene early and prevent the progression of the disease.

Your Health, Our Priority

At Premier Hematology, your health is our top priority. Our dedicated team of healthcare professionals is committed to providing you with personalized, compassionate care every step of the way. From cancer screenings and diagnosis to treatment and survivorship support, we are here to guide you through your cancer journey with expertise, empathy, and unwavering support.
