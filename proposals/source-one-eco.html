What changes for your business

Three things you'll see
in the first 90 days.

Most waste companies just empty your bins. We do something different: we look at what you're actually throwing out, find what has value, and rebuild your setup so you save money and look good doing it. Here's what shows up on your side.

01 · You pay less

Your waste bill goes down — usually by a fifth.

Right now you're paying by weight or by bin pickup. When less of your trash needs hauling to landfill, those numbers drop. We also renegotiate or replace your pickup contract if it's overpriced. Most clients see a lower invoice from month two.

−22%
Typical drop in your monthly hauling bill
02 · You earn back

Cardboard, glass, oil and food scraps become income — not cost.

Recyclers pay for clean, sorted cardboard and metals. Composters take food scraps. Used cooking oil has a market. Most businesses throw all of this in one bin and pay to get rid of it. We help you separate it properly so it goes to the buyer instead of the dump.

$48k
Median yearly savings + revenue, mid-size venue
03 · You look credible

One simple report shows exactly where every kilo of waste went.

Guests ask. Investors ask. Hotel groups and corporate clients increasingly require it. Instead of a vague "we recycle," you get a one-page monthly report with real numbers — what was sorted, what was recovered, what was avoided. Use it on your website, in tenders, or with auditors.

Four simple steps. No disruption to service.

You don't need to know anything about recycling or sustainability to start. We come in, look at what you've got, and walk you through every move. Your kitchen keeps running. Your guests don't notice anything — except, eventually, that you're doing something they care about.

01

We look at your bins.

Free, 30 minutes on-site. We weigh what's in your bins, read your hauling contract, and tell you in plain numbers what you're losing. No commitment.

Right-sized bins where your team already works. Clear labels in your staff's languages. Either we run the sorting on-site, or we collect the mixed waste and sort it at our facility — your choice based on space.

Two ways we can do it

You sort it in-house. Or we take it all and sort it for you.

Same result either way — less landfill, lower bills, recovered value. The difference is just whether the sorting happens at your place or ours. We'll tell you which one fits your space and your team after the free check.

Photo · on-site sorting station

Option A · You sort, we collect

Best for hotels and bigger restaurants with room in the back. Sorting at the source gets you the cleanest material — and the best price for it.

  • We design and install bins that fit your kitchen
  • Labels and quick visual guides in your team's languages
  • We weigh and log every pickup so you know what's there
  • We help swap single-use plastics for compostable versions
Photo · off-site recovery facility

Option B · We collect, we sort

Best if your back-of-house is tight or you run several locations. You keep using one bin like before — we do the heavy lifting on our end.
