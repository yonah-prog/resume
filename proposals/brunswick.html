01

Overview

Brunswick Sports Management has built something genuinely rare in professional sports representation: a firm that operates with the intimacy of a family business and the results of a major agency. Starting with a handful of clients and growing organically to a roster of 30+ athletes across the NBA, NHL, and college sports — without paid marketing, without a big machine behind it — that kind of growth only happens when the work speaks for itself.

The challenge is that the current website doesn't yet reflect the organization Brunswick has become. The social presence is sharp. The track record is real. The relationships are deep. The website needs to catch up — and serve as the centralized hub that ties everything together: the story, the services, the results, and the people behind it all.

This proposal outlines a complete website redesign and build: a modern, professional digital presence that earns trust before the first conversation happens — and gives athletes, families, coaches, and financial advisors a compelling reason to make the call.

The goal in one line: A website that looks and feels like the firm Brunswick already is — not the firm it was three years ago.

What we're solving

  • A website that reflects Brunswick's current scale, reputation, and service offering
  • A clear, compelling story for athletes, families, coaches, and referral partners arriving with different questions
  • Case studies and proof points that turn interest into confidence
  • Content and social media embedded natively — not as an afterthought
  • A platform the Brunswick team can manage and update independently going forward
02

Scope of Work

The project runs across three sequential phases. Strategy comes first — because how the site is structured and what it says matters as much as how it looks. Design follows, then build and launch.

1
Strategy & Structure
Week 1

Before a single design element is placed, we get clear on what the site is saying and to whom. Brunswick's audience isn't one person — it's a college athlete weighing their options, a family navigating a first professional contract, a coach making a referral, a financial advisor looking for a trustworthy partner. The strategy phase ensures the site speaks clearly to all of them.

★ We're already ahead here. Our initial call covered a lot of ground on content strategy, market positioning, and competitive landscape — which means Phase 1 has a significant head start. We're not starting from scratch; we're refining and formalizing what we already aligned on together.

Includes

  • Competitive landscape review: NKFSB, Tribe Star Entertainment, Rubicon, Max Management — where Brunswick differentiates
  • Site architecture and full page map
  • Messaging framework: brand voice, positioning statements, content hierarchy
  • Content strategy — what to include, what to leave out, how much is too much
2
Design
Weeks 1–2

A design that matches the energy and professionalism of Brunswick's social presence — modern, confident, and built for the people they serve. Every page designed desktop and mobile.

Pages

  • Homepage — who they are, what they do, why Brunswick. The full picture at a glance
  • Our Story — the origin, the growth from 3 to 30+, what drives the firm and the people behind it
  • Services — day-to-day athlete management, financial advisory, and concierge services, clearly explained
  • Case Studies — max contracts secured, business ventures launched, athlete milestones. The proof of concept
  • Marketing & Partnerships — how Brunswick supports athletes beyond the contract
  • Contact / Referral — built for how Brunswick actually gets clients: coaches, advisors, and existing clients making introductions

Also includes

  • Social media and video content embedded natively throughout the site
  • Full mobile-first responsive design
3
Build & Launch
Weeks 2–4

Built on WordPress with Elementor — a platform the Brunswick team can update independently without touching code. Fast, clean, and built to last.

  • Full development across all pages
  • CMS setup — add case studies, update content, swap images without a developer
  • Social content integrated and embedded
  • SEO foundations: page titles, meta structure, sitemap, schema markup
  • Speed optimization and cross-browser QA
  • Launch and handover with a walkthrough session for the team
The job isn't done until you're happy — full stop.

Revisions are not counted, capped, or billed separately. We go as many rounds as it takes until the site reflects exactly what Brunswick wants it to be. This is a collaboration, not a transaction — and the standard we're working toward is your complete satisfaction with the final product.
