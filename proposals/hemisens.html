Cardians™

Magnetic signals the EKG can't see

Cardians™ records the magnetic field generated by myocardial cells. Magnetic flux passes through the human body without any distortion — producing spatial readings of cardiac activation that reveal pathology invisible to the standard EKG.

01

Undistorted magnetic flux

Unlike electrical signals, magnetic fields are not altered by tissue, bone, or body geometry. You see what the heart actually produces.

02

Detects vortex & tangential currents

Circular vortex currents — not detectable on EKG — are linked to arrhythmia and ischemia. Cardians™ captures them all.

03

Fully non-invasive and contact-free

No electrodes, no gel, no skin contact. Setup takes seconds, reducing friction at the most time-critical moment of care.

04

Purpose-built for the emergency department

Designed for rapid triage — giving ED physicians the confidence to discharge non-cardiac patients early, without unnecessary testing.

Clinical & Operational Impact

Better outcomes everywhere in the care pathway

The Physics

World-class magnetometry, purpose-built for emergency medicine

Cardians™ is built on over a decade of sensor research at Ben-Gurion University's Sensing Technologies Laboratory — the same team that built the world's most sensitive miniature magnetic sensor and Israel's first commercial military-grade sensor.

01

Ultra-sensitive miniature sensors

Capable of detecting femtotesla-scale fields produced by the beating heart — a level of sensitivity impossible with standard electrodes.

02

Fundamental physics advantage

Magnetic fields obey Maxwell's equations and pass through biological tissue without distortion. EKG cannot achieve this — it's a physics limitation, not an engineering one.

03

Spatial activation mapping

A multi-sensor array produces a 2D map of cardiac activation — resolving the subtle directional deviations caused by myocardial ischemia.

04

Military-grade heritage

The sensor platform was developed for IMOD DDR&D homeland security and space applications — engineered for reliability in the most demanding environments.
