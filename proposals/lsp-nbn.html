Project Scope

Full redesign of 14 slides — Hebrew version

A full visual redesign of the existing Lone Soldiers Program Executive Overview presentation. The goal is to bring the deck to a level that matches the significance of the organization's mission — polished, clear, and built to impress in high-stakes executive conversations.

This is not a light refresh. Every slide will be rethought from the ground up: layout, hierarchy, visual language, and storytelling flow — all rebuilt to function as a cohesive, professional executive presentation.

What the redesign covers

  • Full redesign of all 14 slides — no slide left untouched
  • Layout and information hierarchy optimized for executive-level communication
  • Design system alignment across the full deck — typography, colors, spacing, and visual consistency
  • Refinement of visual storytelling and narrative flow from first slide to last
  • Integration of all existing Hebrew content into a clean, high-end format
  • All assets and imagery formatted, resized, and placed for maximum visual impact
The standard we're aiming for: A deck that communicates confidence and credibility before a single word is read — where the design itself signals that the organization behind it is serious, professional, and worthy of attention.
