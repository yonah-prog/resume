01

Overview

The Lone Soldiers Program operates across a wide range of services, communities, and initiatives — but no single piece of collateral currently captures the full picture. This brochure changes that. Designed as LSP's flagship printed piece, it will serve as the definitive overview of what the program does, who it serves, and why it matters.

Whether placed in the hands of a donor, a potential partner, a government official, or a family arriving in Israel, the brochure needs to communicate scale, credibility, and human impact — in a design language consistent with the executive presentation work already underway.

The goal: One piece that tells the whole LSP story — from the scope of operations to the people behind it — in a format that can be handed across any table in any room.
02

Scope of Work

A complete 8-page brochure designed for print, with a digital PDF version included. All design decisions will align with the visual identity established in the Executive Overview presentation.

Page-by-Page Structure

1
Cover

Bold, high-impact cover that immediately communicates the program's identity and significance. Sets the tone for everything that follows.

2
Who We Are

The LSP mission, origin, and the partnership between Nefesh B'Nefesh and FIDF. The foundation of what the program stands for and why it exists.

3–4
Scope of Operations
Spread

The full breadth of what LSP does — services, support areas, reach, and the communities served. Data, scale, and impact presented clearly across a full spread.

5
Impact & Stories

Human stories and key impact figures — putting faces and outcomes to the program's work. The emotional core of the brochure.

6
Partnerships & Support

How organizations, donors, and individuals can get involved. Partner logos, endorsements, and a clear call to action for engagement.

7
Looking Ahead

Future initiatives, growth plans, and the vision for where LSP is heading — leaving the reader with momentum and a sense of opportunity.

8
Back Cover & Contact

Contact information, website, social handles, and a closing statement. Clean, professional, and actionable.
