01

Overview

This proposal covers two parallel but distinct projects. The first is a complete website redesign for Regional Care Network — replacing an outdated platform with a modern, professional digital presence built to meet today's standards for healthcare organizations. The second is a full rebrand and website rebuild for Jedy Network, starting from the ground up with a cohesive visual identity and a site that reflects it.

Both projects share a common thread: the existing digital presence no longer represents the quality of the work being done. The organizations have matured. The websites haven't. This engagement closes that gap — completely.

The goal in one line: Two organizations, two clean digital transformations — websites and brands that earn credibility before the first conversation happens.

What we're solving

  • Regional Care Network — an outdated platform replaced with a modern, maintainable site that communicates clearly and works across all devices
  • Jedy Network — a brand identity built from scratch: logo, color system, typography, and visual language that gives the organization a coherent, professional look
  • Jedy Network website — a full build on the new brand, matching the scope and quality of the Regional Care Network redesign
  • Both sites built for long-term independence — no developer required for day-to-day content updates
  • Modern design standards throughout: clean layouts, mobile-first, fast load times, and clear calls to action
02

Scope of Work

The engagement is divided into two deliverables running in a coordinated sequence. Regional Care Network launches first, followed immediately by the Jedy Network rebrand and build.

Deliverable 1 — Regional Care Network Website

1
Discovery & Strategy
Week 1

Before design begins, we establish what the site needs to accomplish, who it's speaking to, and how it should be structured. Healthcare audiences — patients, providers, partners, and referring organizations — all come with different questions. The strategy phase ensures the site answers all of them efficiently.

Includes

  • Audience mapping and content hierarchy
  • Site architecture and full page map
  • Messaging framework and brand voice
  • Platform selection: WordPress (recommended for client independence) or an AI-assisted CMS that enables content management through natural language
2
Design
Weeks 1–2

A clean, modern design that communicates trust, clarity, and professionalism. Every page designed desktop and mobile-first.

Core pages

  • Homepage — who Regional Care Network is, what they do, and why it matters
  • Services / Programs — clearly structured service offerings for patients and providers
  • About — the story, mission, and team
  • Referral / Contact — built for how the organization actually receives patients and partners
  • Additional pages as needed based on strategy phase findings
3
Build & Launch
Weeks 2–3

Built on the selected platform with a full CMS setup so the Regional Care Network team can manage content independently going forward.

  • Full development across all pages
  • CMS configuration — update content, add pages, swap images without a developer
  • SEO foundations: page titles, meta descriptions, sitemap, schema markup
  • Speed optimization and cross-browser / cross-device QA
  • Launch and team handover with a walkthrough session

Deliverable 2 — Jedy Network Full Rebrand & Website

1
Brand Identity
Week 3

A complete brand overhaul starting from scratch. Jedy Network gets a full visual identity — everything a modern organization needs to show up consistently and professionally across all channels.

Includes

  • Logo design (primary mark + variations)
  • Color palette — primary, secondary, and neutral system
  • Typography — primary and secondary typeface selection
  • Visual language guidelines: spacing, iconography style, imagery direction
  • Brand usage guide delivered as a PDF reference document
2
Website Design & Build
Weeks 3–4

With the brand established, the website is designed and built entirely within the new identity — the full scope mirrors the Regional Care Network build above.

  • Discovery, architecture, and content strategy (condensed — brand work informs structure)
  • Homepage, About, Services, and Contact pages designed and built
  • CMS setup for team-managed content
  • SEO foundations, QA, and launch
The job isn't done until you're happy — full stop.

Revisions are not counted, capped, or billed separately. We go as many rounds as it takes on both projects until the results reflect exactly what you want them to be. This is a collaboration, not a transaction.
