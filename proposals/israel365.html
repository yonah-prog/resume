Overview

Israel365 is one of the most trusted voices connecting the global Jewish and Christian communities to the Land of Israel. The Israel Truth Report represents an opportunity to build on that trust — creating a dedicated digital hub that serves as the definitive source for accurate, values-driven reporting on Israel in a media landscape increasingly hostile to nuance and truth.

This proposal outlines a comprehensive engagement to take the Israel Truth Report from concept to a fully operational, audience-driven platform. The scope includes strategic positioning, content infrastructure, digital presence, and an ongoing social media engine — built not just to inform, but to mobilize and retain a loyal readership.

The core opportunity: In a moment when trust in mainstream media reporting on Israel is at an all-time low, Israel365 is uniquely positioned to fill that gap — with credibility, reach, and a community already primed to engage.

What this engagement covers

  • Strategic brand and editorial positioning for the Israel Truth Report
  • Full content framework — voice, tone, editorial calendar, and content pillars
  • Website design and build: a purpose-built news/information hub
  • Social media content strategy and management across key platforms
  • Audience growth infrastructure — email, SEO foundations, and community touchpoints
  • Ongoing content production and editorial support
02

Scope of Work

The project is structured across three sequential phases, each building on the last. Phase 1 establishes the strategic and creative foundation. Phase 2 delivers the digital platform. Phase 3 activates and grows the audience through content and social.

1
Strategy & Brand Foundation
Weeks 1–3

Positioning & Editorial Strategy

  • Competitive audit of existing Israel news and advocacy platforms
  • Audience definition: who we're talking to, what they care about, and how to reach them
  • Positioning statement and brand narrative for the Israel Truth Report
  • Defined editorial pillars (e.g. News & Fact-Check, Historical Context, Community Voice, Action Items)
  • Voice and tone guide — consistent across web, email, and social

Visual Identity

  • Logo and visual identity for the Israel Truth Report sub-brand (within Israel365 ecosystem)
  • Color palette, typography, and graphic language
  • Social media visual templates (posts, stories, infographic frames)

Deliverables

  • Brand and editorial strategy document
  • Visual identity kit
  • Social media template library (10+ templates)
2
Website Design & Build
Weeks 3–7

Platform Architecture

  • Site map and user flow design
  • Homepage: hero, latest stories, featured categories, email sign-up CTA
  • Article template: clean, readable layout optimized for long-form and short-form content
  • Category / topic pages (News, Analysis, Video, Resources)
  • About / Mission page and Contact
  • Email newsletter sign-up integration

Technical Build

  • WordPress or equivalent CMS — easy for internal team to update independently
  • Mobile-first, responsive design across all screen sizes
  • SEO foundations: schema markup, meta structure, sitemap, speed optimization
  • Social sharing integration on all article pages
  • Analytics setup (Google Analytics + basic conversion tracking)

Deliverables

  • Fully designed and built website, ready for content population
  • CMS training session for internal team
  • Technical handover documentation
3
Content Production & Social Media Launch
Weeks 7–12 + Ongoing

Launch Content Package

  • 10 launch articles (news analysis, historical context, fact-checks) — written and formatted
  • 3 longform feature pieces establishing editorial authority
  • Welcome email sequence for new subscribers (3-part series)
  • Launch week social media content package (14 posts across platforms)

Ongoing Social Media Management (Monthly Retainer)

  • 20 posts/month across Facebook, Instagram, and X (Twitter)
  • Real-time content response to breaking news and trending topics
  • Monthly editorial calendar — planned 4 weeks in advance
  • Monthly performance report: reach, engagement, follower growth, top content
  • Coordination with Israel365 editorial team for content alignment

Deliverables

  • Full launch content package (articles + social + email)
  • Monthly retainer deliverables as above
  • Quarterly strategy review and optimization session
